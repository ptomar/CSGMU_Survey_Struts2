Computer Science Department

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science. The department offers BS, MS, and PhD programs in Computer Science; MS programs in Software Engineering, Information Systems, and Information Security and Assurance; and a BS program in Applied Computer Science with various concentrations.

Faculty in the department have research interests in networking, architecture, parallel and distributed computing, performance evaluation, software engineering, multimedia, graphics and visualization, databases, software engineering, data mining, security, information systems, artificial intelligence, computer vision, and robotics.

Here is the list of courses provided by Computer science department:

Master Programs and their required courses are:

  • Masters in Computer Science

    1. Data Structures (CS310) or equivalennt.
    2. Formal Methods and Models (CS330) or equivalent.
    3. Computer Systems Architecture including Assembly Programming (CS 367 and CS 465) or equivalent courses.

  • Masters in Information Security and Assurance

    1. ISA 562 Information Security Theory and Practice
    2. ISA 656 Network Security
    3. INFS 612 Principles and Practice of Communication Networks OR CS 555 - Computer Communications and Networking

  • Masters in Information Systems

    1. INFS 612 Principles and Practices of Communication Networks
    2. INFS 614 Database Management
    3. INFS 622 Information Systems Analysis and Design
    4. ISA 562 Information Security Theory and Practice

  • Masters of Software Engineering

    1. SWE 619 Object-Oriented Software Specification and Construction
    2. SWE 621 Software Modeling and Architectural Design
    3. SWE 622 Distributed Software Engineering
    4. SWE 637 Software Testing
